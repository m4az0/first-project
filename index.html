Features

Camera Capture exactly what you see and hear with the new ultra-wide 12 MP camera and five-mic system. Take high-quality photos and immersive videos and share it all to Facebook and Instagram. Audio Seamlessly switch between your favorite tracks, calls, and surroundings with discreet, open-ear speakers. Everything is for your ears only, thanks to improved directional audio.

Meta AI Spark your creativity, get information, and control your glasses just by saying "Hey Meta". Ask Meta AI to get what you need and never miss a beat.
